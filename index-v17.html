01 / The problem

Your AI stops at the edge of each device.

Models keep getting smarter inside the box. Nothing is getting smarter between the boxes. A normal day looks like this.

09:00 / Laptop

You spend an hour briefing your AI on a project.

All of that context is locked to one device.

12:00 / Phone

You step out. Ask one quick question.

Different AI. Knows nothing about your morning. Start over.

19:00 / Home

You walk in. Try to keep going.

A third AI. Knows your name. Nothing about your day.

02 / Why it’s still broken

GPT, Claude, Gemini. Every new model lands on the same wall.

The next upgrade is always about reasoning, context, speed. None of that crosses the device boundary. Your life spans a phone, a laptop, a watch, a car, a home. No single-device assistant can see across them, and the problem grows with every new device you add.

Your life spans

9+ connected devices per person.

Phone, laptop, watch, TV, car, thermostat. No single-device agent can see across them. Source: Deloitte, Parks Associates, 2024.

Context is fragmented

6h 51m of daily screen time, split across devices.

Each screen holds a slice of your day. No one agent sees the whole picture. Source: DataReportal, Backlinko, 2026.

New hardware can’t fix it

$230M burned proving that.

Humane: dead in 10 months. Rabbit R1: 95% abandoned. A new gadget just adds a tenth device that doesn’t talk to the other nine.

Apple proved the shape of the answer

Continuity works. It’s just locked to one brand.

Handoff, AirDrop, Universal Clipboard. The experience works. The rest of your life needs the same thing, across every device you own.

03 / Our belief

The next step change in AI isn’t a bigger model. It’s the layer that connects the ones you already have.

So we stopped trying to build a smarter assistant, and started building the missing tissue between devices.

01

One agent, present on every device you own.

Not a cloud brain driving puppets. Each Orion runs natively on its device, with full local access to screen, files, voice, and tools. The intelligence is in the network between them.

02

Context should follow you, not the device.

What you said to your phone in the morning is in your laptop’s working memory by afternoon. You stop re-briefing. You stop being the connective tissue.

03

You shouldn’t pick the device. The agent should.

Tell Orion once. It chooses which device is best for the task, spins up agents there, and they coordinate directly. You stop being the scheduler.

04 / The product

Orion. Interconnected personal intelligence.

Your life lives across every device you own. Now your AI does too. Tell any device. Any other device already knows. Close the laptop, the context doesn’t die with it.

05 / How it works

Two modes. One layer underneath.

Orion works the way you expect an assistant to work, and the way you wish one could.

Reactive

You pick a device and tell it what to do.

Message any device from any interface, see what it sees, and watch it act in real time. The same conversation can jump from phone to laptop without you restating anything.

Proactive

You tell Orion once. It picks the devices, spins up agents, and they coordinate themselves.

One command. Orion decides which devices are needed, starts the right agents on each, and they talk to each other through the mesh layer. You see the result.
