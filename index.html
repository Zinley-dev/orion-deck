02 / 13

Problem

Today’s agents work in a box.
Not in the world.

They can draft an email while you sit at the keyboard. They can’t take your call, sit in your meeting, or act on your behalf out in the real world.

01 · No personal entity

No phone number, no email. No one can reach it. Nothing tying it to you.

02 · No interconnected layer

Doesn’t see your data across devices. Doesn’t know the people around you. No relationship memory — every person is a stranger.

03 · No continuity

Open a new chat and it forgets everything from the last one.

04 · No real-world reasoning

No social awareness. Doesn’t know who’s asking, who’s in the room, or when it should speak.

03 / 13

Solution

Orion is your world agent.
It acts in the world for you.

Takes the calls you don’t want to take. Joins the meetings you can’t make. Handles the things you’d rather not deal with — and shows up as you.

01 · Personal entity

Orion has its own phone number and email. A real identity people can reach.

02 · Interconnected layer

Knows your data across phone, laptop, and inbox. Knows the people around you. Holds relationship memory — who each person is to you, what’s between you, how they prefer to be reached.

03 · Continuity

Start on your phone in the morning. Pick it up on your laptop at night. Same thread.

04 · Real-world reasoning

Social awareness. Knows who’s asking and who’s in the room. Knows when to speak, when to stay quiet, and how to take part.

Personal entity. Interconnected. Continuity. Real-world reasoning.

How it works · sample flow

Agentic voicemail,
end to end across the owner’s devices.

One example flow. The same setup handles other tasks the owner gives the representative.

01

Install on any device the owner uses.

Phone, laptop, web. Each install becomes a place the representative can run from.

02

Buy a phone number from Orion. Optional

If the owner wants a real-world identity for the rep. A real number, provisioned to the agent. Available in the US and Canada.

03

Calls route to the agent when the owner is busy.

The agent picks up using the owner’s pre-set instructions, asks follow-ups, takes notes, and handles bookings.

04

Recap by email. Reply to continue.

The agent emails the owner a recap of what it did. The owner replies in that thread to keep the conversation going.
