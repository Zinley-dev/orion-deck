04
Our research

We did the research. Then we shipped it.

We’re a research lab. Every core feature in Orion started as research — then became a real, working product.
The problem
Agents have no senses. They’re deaf, blind, and numb.
They can’t see your screen, hear what’s being said, or feel the context of a situation. They only act when explicitly told. No passive awareness — just blind execution waiting for a command.
Research →
Orion Sensor
Gives Orion human-like senses. Eyes — it sees your screen and apps. Ears — it hears conversations and surroundings. Touch — it feels the context of what you’re doing. Always sensing, always aware, knows when to step in without being asked. No polling, no bottleneck.
The problem
No one verifies what it does.
Agents execute actions and hope for the best. No review, no second opinion, no rollback. One unchecked mistake compounds across every step until the whole task fails.
Research →
Multi-Agent Self-Audit
A separate model reviews every action before and after execution. Built-in accountability — Orion checks its own work before anything ships.
The problem
Trapped on one device. Dies when it disconnects.
Every agent lives in one app on one machine. Close the laptop, it’s gone. No way to reach it from your phone, your car, or offline.
Research →
Cross-Device Communication
Orion on your laptop talks to Orion on your phone — encrypted, instant, no cloud middleman. One being that lives across every device you own.
The problem
It doesn’t know you — so it can’t protect you.
Every session starts from scratch. No memory of your preferences, your boundaries, or what’s important to you. Without that context, it can’t tell a critical file from a temp file.
Research →
Deep Personalization & Safety
Orion learns your voice, your contacts, your patterns, your boundaries. The deeper it knows you, the safer it becomes — it won’t delete what matters to you.
These are the first four. We have more in progress.
